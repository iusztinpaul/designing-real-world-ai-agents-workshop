# LinkedIn Post Guideline

## Topic
How to write with AI without losing your voice — avoiding AI slop while keeping your tone, style, and structure intact.

## Angle
Share a practical, opinionated take on what "AI slop" actually is, why most AI-assisted writing sounds the same, and what builders/writers can do to fix it. Draw from personal experience shipping AI writing tools.

## Target Audience
AI engineers, technical content creators, and developers who use LLMs for writing but are frustrated with generic, robotic output.

## Key Points to Cover
- What AI slop is and why it happens (default LLM tendencies, lack of constraints)
- Why most people fail at writing with AI (they prompt wrong — they should define voice, structure, and terminology rules)
- One concrete technique: using profiles/constraints to guide the LLM (structure, terminology, character)
- The result: AI that writes like you, not like a chatbot

## Tone
Direct, slightly controversial. Challenge the "just use ChatGPT" crowd. Share a real opinion on why most AI writing is bad and what to do about it.
